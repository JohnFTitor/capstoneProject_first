The Creative Commons Bootcamp Summer 2022 was born in April 2015, not just as a programming foundation, a school, an Institute, or anything else you can imagine. It was born as a proposition. As an idea to flip the world mindset around what programming is and means to Science, Industry, Future, and finally to the most important actor: People. This project aims to show that when we program with a purpose, we do more than code. We develop. And if you have that mindset, this place is what you might call "Home".
